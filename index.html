How it works

From online move to physical piece — in milliseconds

The Voltplay board bridges the digital and physical through a tight hardware-software loop that most players never even think about.

Opponent moves online

A move is played on Chess.com, Lichess, or against our AI. The move is transmitted to the board via Wi-Fi in under 20ms.

Board calculates path

The onboard processor plans the safest XY actuator path to move the piece without disturbing adjacent squares.

Magnetic actuator fires

The hidden actuator slides the piece to the destination square with a satisfying magnetic click. Captured pieces move to the side pocket automatically.

LEDs confirm & coach

The destination square illuminates. If coaching mode is on, the board highlights the engine's preferred reply and any tactical alerts.
